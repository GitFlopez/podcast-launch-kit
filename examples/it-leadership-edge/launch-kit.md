## SHOW ART BRIEF

**Concept:** Dark background (deep charcoal or navy), single central image of a circuit board pattern that transforms into a leadership graphic (organizational chart or city grid). Clean, professional, slightly edgy. The viewer should feel "this is serious" before they read a word.

**Color Palette:**
- #1A1F36 (Deep navy) — authority, technology, depth
- #0CF2A4 (Electric teal/green) — innovation, action, "edge"
- #FFFFFF (White) — text, clarity, contrast
- #2D3561 (Mid-blue) — secondary, depth layer

**Typography:**
- "THE IT LEADER'S" — condensed sans-serif, all caps, medium weight, white
- "EDGE" — same font, dramatically larger, electric teal, stands alone on its own line
- "with Marcus Rivera" — smaller, light weight, beneath the show title

**Text placement:** Top third = show name, bottom third = host name, center = visual element

**Reference shows:** Darknet Diaries (dark/moody), How I Built This (clean/professional), Acquired (premium/dark)

**What to avoid:**
1. Stock photo of a person in a suit (looks generic)
2. Blue gradient background (every tech podcast does this)
3. Microphone as the central image (overdone)
