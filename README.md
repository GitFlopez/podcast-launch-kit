# Podcast Launch & Growth System Generator

**Build a podcast people actually find and listen to — from concept to 1,000 downloads.**

---

## The Problem This Solves

30,000+ new podcasts launch every month. 90% never reach 1,000 downloads per episode. Not because the content is bad — because the strategy is missing.

Most podcast creators:
- Pick a name without researching competitors
- Launch with 1 episode and wonder why nobody finds them
- Write show descriptions nobody reads
- Post on social and get 12 plays from friends and family
- Quit by Episode 8

This skill generates the complete system: concept positioning that stands out, a 12-episode content calendar, a multi-platform launch kit (Apple Podcasts + Spotify listing copy + 18 social posts), and a 12-month growth + monetization roadmap.

## What You Get (4 Prompts)

### Prompt 1 — Podcast Concept & Positioning *(Free)*
- 5 podcast name candidates with domain likelihood
- 3 tagline options
- Complete target listener profile (demographics, psychographics, pain points)
- Unique angle statement (what no competitor offers)
- Competitive landscape analysis on a 2×2 matrix
- Apple Podcasts category + keyword recommendations
- Episode format and length recommendation

### Prompt 2 — Trailer Script + 12-Episode Calendar
- Full 90-120 second trailer script (ready to record)
- 30-second social teaser cut
- 12-episode content calendar (2 title options + description + SEO keyword per episode)
- First 3 full episode outlines (cold open → segments → close)
- 4 recurring segment ideas that build listener habit

### Prompt 3 — Multi-Platform Launch Kit
- 30-task pre-launch checklist (5 critical tasks flagged)
- Apple Podcasts listing: title, subtitle, full 4,000-character description, 10 keywords
- Spotify listing copy (200-char search + 1,000-char full)
- Show art creative brief (color palette, typography, AI generation prompt)
- 18 pieces of launch week social content (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Email, Reddit)
- Launch day hour-by-hour posting schedule

### Prompt 4 — Growth Playbook + Monetization Roadmap
- 10 guest acquisition target profiles + outreach templates (email, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- 5 cross-promotion swap targets + pitch scripts
- Listener-to-subscriber funnel (lead magnet + 5-email welcome sequence)
- SEO show notes strategy (10 posts ranked by search opportunity)
- Month 3/6/12 growth milestones with leading indicators
- Monetization ladder: affiliate → paid community → sponsorships
- Full sponsorship rate card (CPM-based, by download tier)
- Revenue projections at 1K / 5K / 10K downloads/episode
- 5 growth hacks ranked by effort vs. impact
