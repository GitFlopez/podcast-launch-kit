## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete system for launching and growing a podcast from zero. Four specialized prompts cover every stage: concept + positioning, content calendar + episode outlines, multi-platform launch kit, and growth + monetization playbook.

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Podcast Concept & Positioning (FREE)
**Input:** Host's expertise, target listener, 3-5 topic areas, 3 competing podcasts  
**Output:**
- Show concept statement (one paragraph)
- Podcast name: 5 candidates with domain availability check format
- Tagline: 3 options (under 10 words each)
- Target listener profile (ICP): demographics, psychographics, top 3 pain points, why they'd subscribe
- Unique angle statement: what this podcast does that no competitor does
- Category + subcategory recommendation for Apple Podcasts + Spotify
- Competitive landscape analysis: 3 competitor shows mapped on Authority vs. Accessibility matrix
- Episode format recommendation: length, structure, interview vs. solo vs. panel

### Prompt 2 — Trailer Script + First 12 Episodes
**Input:** Show concept from Prompt 1, host's biggest insight/story, 12 episode topic ideas  
**Output:**
- Trailer script (90-120 seconds): hook + promise + who it's for + call to subscribe
- Teaser cut (30 seconds): for social media / paid ads
- 12-episode content calendar with:
  - Episode title (2 options per episode)
  - 3-sentence description
  - Key takeaway / listener promise
  - Guest or solo format recommendation
  - SEO keyword target for show notes
- First 3 full episode outlines (hook → segments → close → CTA structure)
- Recurring segment ideas (add consistency that drives return listeners)

### Prompt 3 — Multi-Platform Launch Kit
**Input:** Show name, launch date, host social handles, primary distribution platforms  
**Output:**
- Pre-launch checklist (30 tasks in 4 phases: 30/14/7 days out + launch day)
- Apple Podcasts listing copy: title, subtitle (max 255 chars), description (4,000 chars), keywords
- Spotify listing copy: description + category tags
- Show art brief: color palette, typography style, composition notes for designer or AI image generator
- Launch week social content:
  - 7 Instagram/Facebook posts (captions + image concept)
  - 5 LinkedIn posts (thought leadership angle)
  - 5 tweets/X posts
  - 1 email to existing list (newsletter format)
  - 1 Reddit/Facebook Group announcement (no-spam format)
- Launch day sequence: exact posting schedule with platform, time, and copy

### Prompt 4 — Growth Playbook + Monetization Roadmap
**Input:** Show concept, current audience size (email list, social following), 12-month goal  
**Output:**
- Guest acquisition system: 10 outreach targets with personalization angles + pitch template
- Cross-promotion strategy: 5 shows to pitch for swap episodes + swap pitch script
- Listener conversion funnel: podcast → email list → paid offer (3-step sequence)
- SEO strategy: 10 show notes blog posts ranked by search volume opportunity
- Month 3-6-12 growth milestones with leading indicators (downloads, email subscribers, reviews)
- Monetization ladder: Episode 1 → CPM ads → affiliate → paid community → course → DFY service
  - Revenue projections at 1K / 5K / 10K downloads/episode
  - Sponsorship rate card (pre-roll/mid-roll/post-roll CPM formula)
  - Minimum viable audience for first sponsor (500-1,000 downloads/episode rule)
- 5 growth hacks ranked by effort vs. impact: repurposing to TikTok/Reels, audiogram strategy, guest swap, SEO show notes, email list building from episodes
