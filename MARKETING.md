## Differentiation Strategy

### 1. The Full Lifecycle System
Every competitor tool covers one piece (hosting, editing, listing). This skill covers the complete lifecycle from first idea to first sponsor. Nobody else at any price point assembles all 4 stages in one workflow.

### 2. Platform-Specific Copy
Apple Podcasts has a 255-character subtitle field, 4,000-character description field, and a separate keywords field. Spotify shows 200 characters in search and 1,000 in full. This skill generates copy for each field, each platform — most guides ignore the difference.

### 3. The Monetization Roadmap
Most podcast "strategy" stops at "launch it and it will grow." This skill includes a CPM-based sponsor rate card, a minimum viable audience threshold by niche, and revenue projections at 1K/5K/10K downloads. Buyers can see the business case before they record episode one.

### 4. Guest Acquisition as a System
Guest swaps are the #1 growth tactic for podcasts under 1,000 downloads/episode. No competitor skill or tool generates a guest pipeline with outreach scripts, target guest profiles, and swap pitch templates.
