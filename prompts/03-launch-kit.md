# Prompt 3 — Multi-Platform Launch Kit

## Instructions for Use
Run Prompts 1 and 2 first. Paste the show name, tagline, and launch date below.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a podcast marketing specialist. I have my show concept locked and my first episodes recorded. Now I need a complete multi-platform launch kit to go from zero to visible in my first 30 days.

SHOW NAME: [From Prompt 1]
TAGLINE: [From Prompt 1]
HOST NAME: [Your name and short title — e.g., "Marcus Rivera, IT Director"]
LAUNCH DATE: [Exact date — e.g., "May 15, 2026"]
LAUNCH EPISODES: [How many episodes dropping on day 1 — typically 3]
TARGET LISTENER: [From Prompt 1 — 2-3 sentence summary]
HOST SOCIAL HANDLES:
  - LinkedIn: [handle or N/A]
  - Instagram: [handle or N/A]
  - Twitter/X: [handle or N/A]
  - TikTok: [handle or N/A]
EXISTING AUDIENCE: [Email list size / Social following / Community memberships — be honest]
PODCAST HOSTING PLATFORM: [Buzzsprout / Transistor / Podbean / Spotify for Podcasters / other]

---

Generate my complete launch kit:

**SECTION 1: PRE-LAUNCH CHECKLIST (30 Tasks)**

Organize by timeline: 30 days out, 14 days out, 7 days out, launch day, week after launch.

For each task:
- [ ] [Task]
- Why it matters (1 sentence)
- Time required: [X minutes/hours]

Include tasks across: recording/editing, show art, hosting setup, distribution (Apple/Spotify/Amazon/iHeart), social profiles, website/landing page, email list setup, pre-launch buzz, launch day posts, review asks.

Flag the 5 most critical tasks with ⚡ — if you only do 5 things, do these.

**SECTION 2: APPLE PODCASTS LISTING COPY**

Write fully optimized listing copy:

- **Show Title:** [Exact title as it will appear — consider keyword placement]
- **Author Field:** [Your name — this is a searchable field]
- **Subtitle (max 255 characters):** [SEO-optimized, natural language, primary keyword + benefit]
- **Description (up to 4,000 characters):**
  - Opening hook (2 sentences) — written for the listener, not for the algorithm
  - Who the show is for (3-4 bullet points using your target listener profile)
  - What they'll learn/gain (3-4 bullet points)
  - Host credibility paragraph (3-4 sentences, specific achievements, not resume)
  - Episode examples: "Recent episodes include..." (3 examples from your calendar)
  - Subscribe CTA
  - Website/contact link
- **Keywords:** 10 keywords for the keyword field (Apple uses these for search)
- **Episode Numbering:** Recommendation on episodic vs. serial format

**SECTION 3: SPOTIFY LISTING COPY**

- **Show Description:** Spotify shows 200 characters in search, 1,000 characters in full — write both versions
- **Category Tags:** Primary + secondary recommendation
- **Language + Country settings**
- **Explicit content flag:** [Recommendation]

**SECTION 4: SHOW ART BRIEF**

Write a creative brief for podcast cover art (1400x1400px):
- **Concept:** [Visual concept in 2-3 sentences — describe the image, not the feeling]
- **Color Palette:** 3-4 hex codes with rationale (what emotion each color signals)
- **Typography Style:** Font personality (bold + condensed? Serif authority? Handwritten warmth?)
- **Text Elements:** Show name + tagline + host name placement
- **Background treatment:** Photo, illustration, gradient, or abstract
- **Reference shows:** 3 shows whose art is in a similar visual direction (explain what to borrow)
- **What to avoid:** 3 common mistakes in this category's podcast art
- **AI generation prompt:** A DALL-E/Midjourney/Ideogram prompt that will produce a usable starting point

**SECTION 5: LAUNCH WEEK SOCIAL CONTENT (18 Pieces)**

Write fully copy-ready content for launch week. No templates — actual words.

**Instagram/Facebook (7 Posts):**
- Day -7: Announcement post [Caption + image concept]
- Day -3: Behind-the-scenes / why I'm launching this [Caption + image concept]
- Day -1: "Tomorrow" teaser [Caption + image concept]
- Launch Day AM: Go live post [Caption + image concept]
- Launch Day PM: Thank you / momentum post [Caption + image concept]
- Day +3: Episode 1 highlight quote [Caption + image concept]
- Day +7: First week recap + listener shoutout [Caption + image concept]

Each post: full caption (150-300 words), 5-8 relevant hashtags, image/graphic concept description.

**LinkedIn (5 Posts):**
- Week before: Personal story post — why I'm starting this podcast
- Launch day: Professional announcement (3-5 paragraphs, no hashtag spam)
- Day +2: Key insight from Episode 1 (thought leadership angle)
- Day +5: Lesson learned from recording the trailer (vulnerability/process post)
- Day +10: Early listener response / momentum post

Each post: full copy, optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm (no links in first comment, break up paragraphs).

**Twitter/X (5 Posts):**
- Announcement thread (3 tweets)
- Episode 1 quote card tweet
- Episode 2 announcement tweet
- Engagement tweet (question to target audience)
- Milestone tweet (first 100 plays, etc.)

**Email to Existing List (1 Email):**
- Subject line: 3 options (curiosity / benefit / personal)
- Preview text: 2 options
- Body: 300-400 words (personal, not corporate)
- CTA: Subscribe link + "forward this to someone who needs it" ask

**Reddit/Facebook Group Post (1 Post):**
- Non-spammy announcement for relevant communities
- Leads with value, not promotion
- Ends with genuine invitation

**SECTION 6: LAUNCH DAY POSTING SCHEDULE**

Hour-by-hour schedule for launch day:
| Time | Platform | Content | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
[Build a complete launch day schedule from 7AM to 9PM, including: what to post where, when to check reviews, when to post thank-yous to early listeners, when to send the email]
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Launch with 3 episodes (not 1) — gives new listeners somewhere to go after they finish episode 1
- The Apple Podcasts description is the highest-leverage copywriting you'll do — spend 30 min on it
- Show art is disproportionately important — listeners judge by cover before they ever hear a word
- The pre-launch checklist is designed to be exhaustive — you won't do all 30 things, but you should know what you're skipping
